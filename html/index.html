Recipes Rice: How to make it:

Recipe for making california sprouted rice follow:

If you prefer slightly al dente rice:

Combine 1 1/4 cups of rice with 2 cups of water or desired broth and 1 Tbsp olive oil.

Bring to a boil and stir once to mix.

Reduce heat to low, cover with a tight-fitting lid and cook for 25 minutes.

Remove from heat and let stand for 5 minutes.

Fluff with a fork and serve.

If you prefer softer rice:

Increase liquid by 1/2 cup and cook time by 5 minutes.

Recipe for making white rice

Combine 1 cup of rice with 2 cups of water and 1 Tbsp olive oil.

Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to the lowest setting.

Cook for about 18 minutes.
